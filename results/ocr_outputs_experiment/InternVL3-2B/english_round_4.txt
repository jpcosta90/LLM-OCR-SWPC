Hydrogen (¹₁₃H) has three naturally occurring isotopes: ¹²H, and ¹³H.¹¹H and ²H and ³H are stable, while ³H has a half-life of 12.32 years. Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zeptosecond (10⁻²¹ s).
