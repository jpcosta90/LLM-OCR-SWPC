Hydrogen (¹H) has three naturally occurring isotopes: ¹H, ²H, and ³H. ¹H and ²H are stable, while ³H has a half-life of 12.32 years. [4][nb 1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 epf (10⁻²¹ s). [5][6]
