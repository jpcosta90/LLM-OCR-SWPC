Isotopes of hydrogen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

1H, 2H, and 3H have three naturally occurring isotopes: ¹H, and ²H. ¹H and ²H are stable, while ³H has a ½-life of 12.32 years. [4][nb 1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a ½-life of less than 1 zetostcent (10⁻²¹ s). [5][6]
