**Isotopes of hydrogen**

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

---

**Hydrogen (1H)**

Has three naturally occurring isotopes: \( ^1H \), \( ^2H \), and \( ^3H \). \( ^1H \) and \( ^2H \) are stable, while \( ^3H \) has a half-life of 12.32 years. [4][nb 1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zep-topisecond (10^-21 s). [5][6]
