Isotopes of hydrogen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hydrogen (¹H), having three naturally occurring isotopes: ¹H, and ³H. ¹H and ³H are stable, while ³H has a half-life of 12.32 years. [4][nb=1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zeptosecond (10⁻²s). [5][6]
