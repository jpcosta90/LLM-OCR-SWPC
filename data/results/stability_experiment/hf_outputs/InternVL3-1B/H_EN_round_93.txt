**Isotopes of hydrogen**

Hydrogen (¹H) has three naturally occurring isotopes:  
- ¹H;  
- ²H;  
- and  
³H.  
1H and 2H are stable, while ³H has a half-life of 12.32 years.[^1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 eptosecm (10⁻²¹ s).
