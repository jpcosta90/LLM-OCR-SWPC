Isotopes of hydrogen

---

Hydrogen (1H) has three naturally occurring isotopes: ¹H, ²H, and ³H. ¹H and ²H are stable, while ³H has a half-life of 12.32 years. [4] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1ゼpteoscopic (10⁻²¹ s). [5][6]
