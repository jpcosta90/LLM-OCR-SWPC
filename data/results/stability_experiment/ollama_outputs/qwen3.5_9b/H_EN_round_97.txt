Hydrogen (_1H_) has three naturally occurring isotopes: _1H_, _2H_, and _3H_. _1H_ and _2H_ are stable, while _3H_ has a half-life of 12.32 years.[4][nb 1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zeptosecond (10^-21 s).[5][6]
