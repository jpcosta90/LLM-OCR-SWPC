Science is a systematic discipline that builds and organises knowledge in the form of testable hypotheses or predictions about universes.[149] [72] Modern scientific study often begins with an explanation for one phenomenon being made, which then leads to further investigations into more explanations. This process typically involves making observations , forming questions from these observa tions  and testing possible answers through the formulation of theories .
