The term "science" is defined as systematic knowledge in any form that can be tested and verified through experiment or observation. [ citation needed ] In this definition, scientific inquiry refers to the process by which scientists develop explanations for natural phenomena with testable hypotheses (experiments) . Theories are a set of principles within science , designed so they allow new observations to explain an existing theory when applied properly.

Since its formal establishment in Europe during the 17th century as distinct disciplines, such knowledge has been categorised into many branches. [ citation needed ] Some fields have developed natural philosophy (philosophy), social sciences and mathematics .
