**Science ** is a systematic discipline that builds and organises knowledge in the form of testable hypotheses [3] about the universe. [149 ][72][158 ] Modern science (also known as natural science) is typically divided into six major branches: astronomy , biology chemistry physics Earth sciences mathematics social studies . These disciplines are often referred to together as STEM fields or "hard" and study physical phenomena, while their counterparts in the humanities [3]—such philosophy of nature which were made during antiquity[158 ]( science), sociology etc. —are considered separate because they rely on deductive reasoning insteadof scientific methodas themain methodology.[72][906]( 4) Meanwhile , applied sciences are disciplines that use existing knowledge to solve practical problems .
