Hydrogen is the only element

²H is deuterium[7] and ³H is tritium,[8] The symbols D and T are
sometimes used for deuterium and tritium; IUPAC
accepts said symbols, but recommends the standard isotopic
symbols ²H and ³H, to avoid confusion in alphabetic sorting of
chemical formulas.[9]
Hydrogen has three naturally occurring
