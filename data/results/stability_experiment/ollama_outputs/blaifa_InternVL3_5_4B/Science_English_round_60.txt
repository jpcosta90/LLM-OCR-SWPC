Science is a systematic discipline that builds and organises knowledge in the form of testable hypotheses and predictions about the universe.[a] Modern science typically enters into two or three – major branches;[b][c] The natural sciences, which study the physical world,[d]  and the social sciences , which study individuals and societies. While referred to as "the formal sciences", the study of logic mathematics, s cience theories (such as engineering) are typically regarded as separate because they rely on deductive reasoning instead of the scientific method[as main source][e]
