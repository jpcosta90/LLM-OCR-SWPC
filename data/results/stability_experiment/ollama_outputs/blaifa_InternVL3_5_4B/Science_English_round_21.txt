**Science ** is a systematic discipline that builds and organises knowledge in the form of testable hypotheses *and predictions* about the universe. [14] ^{[7][2]} Modern science is typically split into two or three major branches: the natural sciences, which study the physical world , and the social sciences, which study individuals and societies . While commonly referred to as formal sciences, the study of logic , mathematics , computer theory *and* philosophy are all regarded as separate because they rely on deductive reasoning instead of the scientific method as their main methodology. [6][7] ^{[19]} Meanwhile, applied engineering is disciplines that include knowledge derived from science and  physics.*
