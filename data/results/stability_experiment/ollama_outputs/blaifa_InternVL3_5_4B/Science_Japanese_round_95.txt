 scientific knowledge includes the following main sub-disciplines:

- physics: studying matter and its motion, often including gravity
- chemistry : analyzing substances at a molecular level. 
- biology: describing life.
- earth science: understanding our planet's surface of Earth in particular。
  
  These are referred to as physical sciences.

1. It deals with abstract entities (numbers and shapes).
2. Mathematics does not rely on experimental evidence.
3. The concepts in mathematical theories are derived from logic alone.
