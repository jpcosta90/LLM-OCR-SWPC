Isotopes of hydrogen

An isotopologue (from Greek: ἴσος isos "equal", τόπων topōn “position”) refers to a group 
of chemical compounds or elements consisting of atoms with identical position in space but differing nuclear composition.

Hydrogen atom. The nucleus contains one proton, and an electron orbits the hydrogen (proton) by
quantum mechanics.
