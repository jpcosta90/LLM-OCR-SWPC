Hydrogen (`1H`, `2`, and `3H`). `1H` and `2H` are naturally occurring isotopes; all `3H` have a half-life of 12.32 years. `1` heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zep-tosecond (10⁻²¹ s).
