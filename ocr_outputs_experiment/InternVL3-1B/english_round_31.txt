Isotopes of hydrogen  
Isotope 1H has three naturally occurring isotopes: ¹H, and ³H. ¹H has a half-life of 12.32 years.[4][nb 1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zebtosec (10⁻²₁ s).[5][6]
