Isotopes of hydrogen
From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hydrogen (¹H) has three naturally occurring isotopes: ¹, ²H, and ³H. ¹H and ²H are stable, while ³H has a half-life of 12.32 years. [4][nb 1]  Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zeptosecond (10⁻²¹ s). [5][6]
