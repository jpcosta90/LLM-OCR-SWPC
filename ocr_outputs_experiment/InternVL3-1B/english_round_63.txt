Isotopes of hydrogen  
-------------------  
From the free encyclopedia

Hydrogen (1H) has three naturally occurring isotopes:  
1H, 2H, and 3H.  
1H has a half-life of 12.32 years.[4]  
Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1epterosec (10^-21 s).[5]
