Isotopes of hydrogen

Hydrogen ( 1H ), has three naturally occurring isotopes: 1H, 2H, and 3H. 1H and 2H are stable, while 3H has a half-life of 12.32 years. [4][nb 1] Heavier isotopes also exist; all are synthetic and have a half-life of less than 1 zetacept (10⁻²¹ s). [5][6]
